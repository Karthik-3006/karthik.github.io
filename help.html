Game Details:

  • After entering into the website you will see Rock, Paper. Scissors options.
  • You can select any of them and computer pick any of them randomly.
  • Your selection: Rock , Computer selection: Rock - Tie
    ---Your selection: Rock , Computer selection: Paper - You lose
    ---Your selection: Rock , Computer selection: Scissors - You win
    ---Your selection: Paper , Computer selection: Rock - You won
    ---Your selection: Paper , Computer selection: Paper - Tie
    ---Your selection: Paper , Computer selection: Scissors - You lose
    ---Your selection: Scissors , Computer selection: Rock - You lose
    ---Your selection: Scissors , Computer selection: Paper - You win
    ---Your selection: Scissors , Computer selection: Scissors - Tie

Instructions:

  • You have only 5 chances to play the game.
  • After finishing the game click Reset button to play again.
